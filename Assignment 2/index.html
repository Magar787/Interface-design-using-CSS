About Hershey Chocolates

Hershey Chocolate, an iconic name in the world of confectionery, embodies a rich heritage of craftsmanship and innovation spanning over a century. Renowned for its quality and unmistakable flavor, Hershey's chocolate products range from classic milk chocolate bars to indulgent assortments like Hershey's Kisses, each offering a taste experience cherished by millions worldwide. Beyond its delectable offerings, Hershey is synonymous with community and sustainability, committed to ethically sourcing ingredients and supporting cocoa farming communities globally. Founded in 1894 by Milton S. Hershey in Lancaster, Pennsylvania, USA, the company has a legacy rooted in passion and commitment to excellence. With a vision to create affordable, high-quality chocolate for the masses, Hershey continues to delight generations with its beloved chocolates, making every moment sweeter and more memorable.
